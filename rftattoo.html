Project RFTattoo: A Wireless Platform for Speech Recognition



3 million people in the US are suffering voice impairments. Current solutions, like keyboard and electrolarynx, require extra effort for the patients to compensate for their voice disorders. We present RFTattoo an elegant speech recognition system for voice impaired using batteryless and flexible RFID tattoos.
